    Return Policy

    Home / Return Policy

    Last updated: June 2026

    14-Day Return Window

    You have 14 calendar days from the date of delivery to initiate a return. Items must be received back within 7 days of receiving your RMA number. Requests made after 14 days will not be accepted.

    Condition Requirements

    Returned items must be unused, in their original packaging, with all tags attached. Items showing signs of wear, use, or damaged packaging may be subject to a restocking fee or rejection. We reserve the right to refuse returns that do not meet these conditions.

    Return Process

    1. Contact our support team at hello@sollene.site with your order number and reason for return.
    2. Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and return shipping instructions.
    3. Pack the item securely in its original packaging, include the RMA number, and ship to the provided address.
    4. Once received and inspected, your refund will be processed within 5-10 business days.

    Refund Timeline

    Refunds are processed within 5-10 business days after we receive and inspect the returned item. The refund will be issued to your original payment method. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed. Please note that your financial institution may take additional time to post the refund.

    Exclusions

    The following items are not eligible for return: intimate apparel, swimwear, opened personal care products (deodorants, skincare), sale items, gift cards, and items marked as final sale. Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a defective or incorrect item.

    Need help with a return? Contact us at hello@sollene.site
