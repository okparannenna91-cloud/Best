SOLLENE - MVP PRODUCT SHOWCASE WEBSITE

Build a modern, production-ready product showcase website called SOLLENE for a small retail business that sells household items, clothing, deodorants, personal care products, and general lifestyle products.

====================================
BRAND INFORMATION
=================

Brand Name:
SOLLENE

Domain:
sollene.site

Tagline:
Elevating Everyday Living

Brand Style:

* Premium
* Minimal
* Elegant
* Trustworthy
* Modern

The website should feel like a premium lifestyle brand rather than a generic online store.

The primary goal is to allow customers to browse products and place orders directly through WhatsApp.

There should be:

* NO customer registration
* NO customer login
* NO shopping cart
* NO checkout system
* NO payment gateway
* NO customer dashboard
* NO email system

Customer Journey:

1. Visit website
2. Browse products
3. View product details
4. Contact via WhatsApp
5. Place order manually
